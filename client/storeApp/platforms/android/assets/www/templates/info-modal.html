Welcome to storeApp!

Use the Account tab to create an account or sign in with your Google account. Once you're signed in, you can add tags to search for and/or block in the Shop tab.

Use the Shop tab to search for specific products or to search by your categories/filters you set in your Account tab. To add a product to your favourites (listed on the Favs tab), simply slide the product to the left to reveal the Fav button.

Use the Favs tab to manage your favourite items. To remove an item, just slide it to the left and click the X button.
